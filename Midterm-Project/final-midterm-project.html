THE 2024 STICKY MONSTER FESTIVAL IS HERE!

THE LONGEST RUN

Looking for a meaningful way to engage with your community while staying active and promoting sustainability? Join The Longest Run, the Sticky Monster Fest's brand new benefit event. It combines the thrill of running with the opportunity to give back to the community and support environmental sustainability efforts. Sticky monsters will have the chance to lace up their sneakers, hit the pavement, and make a positive impact.

WHERE, WHEN, WHY

The Annual Sticky Monster Festival is an experience that brings sticky monsters together to explore new skills, to socialize, to get active, and to make long-lasting memories! Whether you are travelling solo or with your sticky family and friends, all monsters are encouraged to express themselves whether it's by showcasing your unique talents in one of the mainstage showcases, participating in sports events or if it's by setting up personal booths and shops.

We can't wait to see you there!
